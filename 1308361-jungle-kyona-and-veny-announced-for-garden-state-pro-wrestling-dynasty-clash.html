Jungle Kyona And VENY Announced For Garden State Pro Wrestling Dynasty Clash

Publish date: 2024-06-30

Jungle Kyona is coming to .

In a tweet on July 17, the promotion announced that Kyona will be at its third show, Dynasty Clash, which will be held in the Warinanco Sports Center in Roselle, New Jersey, on September 16.

Kyona will be touring the United States this fall. She had been sidelined with numerous injuries, including an ACL rupture, in the fall of 2020. Kyona previously had a successful run in Stardom. She recently returned to the ring at the Hana Kimura Memorial show in May. In addition to GSPW, she has been announced for West Coast Pro Wrestling’s Ride The Lightning event on October 8th, as well as two upcoming shows with Deadlock Pro Wrestling.

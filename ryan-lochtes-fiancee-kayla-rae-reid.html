Who Is Ryan Lochte's Wife Kayla Rae Reid?

Publish date: 2024-08-15

As most people know by now, Lochte's professional swimming career practically fell apart during the 2016 Olympic Games in Rio when his story about getting robbed at gunpoint turned out to be a big, fat lie. Fortunately for him, his relationship endured, due in great part to Reid's loyal support. "I will always support him no matter what," she said in video footage obtained by the Daily Mail in August 2016.

Reid's enduring affection also played a key role after protesters stormed the stage during Lochte's Dancing With the Stars debut in September 2016. "She's definitely just been so supportive," Lochte said of Reid (via People magazine). Lochte later told USA Today that he knew Reid was "the one" when she stuck by him through the scandal. "She still loves me and she's still right there with me," he said.

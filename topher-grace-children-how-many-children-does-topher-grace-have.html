Topher Grace Children: How many children does Topher Grace Have?

Publish date: 2024-07-24

Topher Grace is an American actor known for his work in television and film. Topher and his wife Ashley Hinshaw have three kids.

On August 1, 2017, they announced that they were expecting their first child.  On November 1, 2017, their daughter  Mabel Jane was born.

Ashley Hinshaw announced on January 5, 2020, that she and her husband Topher were expecting their second child together; their kid was born in 2020.

Topher Grace revealed on The Kelly Clarkson Show on September 30, 2022, that he and Hinshaw were expecting their third child together. The names of their other two kids are not public knowledge.

Topher Grace gained widespread recognition for his role as Eric Forman in the popular television series “That ’70s Show,” which aired from 1998 to 2006.

In addition to his television work, Topher Grace has appeared in various films. He is perhaps best known for his role as Eddie Brock/Venom in the 2007 film “Spider-Man 3,” which was part of the Sam Raimi-directed Spider-Man trilogy.

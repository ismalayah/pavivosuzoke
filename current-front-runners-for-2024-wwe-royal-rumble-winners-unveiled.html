Current Front Runners for 2024 WWE Royal Rumble Winners Unveiled

Publish date: 2024-05-17

The 2024 Royal Rumble will take place on Saturday, January 27th, 2024, at Tropicana Field in St. Petersburg, Florida. Tickets are already shutting down the MLB box office website, and some fans are already placing their bets.

The 2024 WWE Royal Rumble, slated for January, has not yet disclosed any match details or participants. That being said, Bet Online has odds for who will win the big men and women’s matches in January 2024.

The early betting odds are available, and the front runners in the men’s category are LA Knight and The Rock, with Nia Jax and Raquel Rodriguez leading the odds in the women’s division. It is interesting that The Rock is there, since we have previously reported that WWE has no current match booked for The Great One.

That being said, LA Knight and The Rock share the top spot with odds of 5/2 (+250), followed by Gunther at 4/1, Drew McIntyre and Jey Uso at 5/1 each, and Sami Zayn and Cody Rhodes at 5/1 and 6/1, respectively.

In the 2024 Women’s Royal Rumble Winner category, the betting odds show that Nia Jax and Raquel Rodriguez share the leading position with odds of 3/1. Following closely, there are several wrestlers with odds of 4/1, including Becky Lynch, Iyo Sky, and Rhea Ripley. Bianca Belair and Charlotte Flair both have odds of 5/1, while Asuka holds 6/1 odds. Further down the list, Bayley stands at 10/1, and Jade Cargill has odds of 12/1.

Next year’s January premium live event will be the second Royal Rumble to be held at the stadium after the 2021 event, though the first with live fans in attendance due to the COVID-19 pandemic that year. This time, fans will be a huge X factor for the show that was missing in 2021. Only time will tell who ends the night pointing at the WrestleMania sign.

You can check out the full betting odds in the space below. As always, please bet responsibly, because you never know what can happen as WWE kicks off the road to WrestleMania 40.

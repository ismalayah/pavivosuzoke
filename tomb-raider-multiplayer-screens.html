Tomb Raider Multiplayer Screens

Publish date: 2024-07-15
Crystal Dynamics have already revealed that multiplayer will be a part of the upcoming Tomb Raider. Zac Levi confidently gave us the run down in the recent Dev Diary where we were able to pay witness to the final hours of game development, and took a look at the multiplayer addition.

A few new screens have been released today showcasing one of the maps called 'Chasm' in action, as well as a screen of the multiplayer lobby where players can customize their loadouts, choose their character and invite their friends to join them:



Players can get to grips with Tomb Raider and its multiplayer addition when the game releases on March 5th.

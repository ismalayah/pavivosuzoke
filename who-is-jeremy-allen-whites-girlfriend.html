Who is Jeremy Allen Whites girlfriend?

Publish date: 2024-06-04

After gaining Hollywood traction for his big-time break in Showtime’s Shameless, actor Jeremy Allen White is undoubtedly dominating headlines and quickly becoming one of the most popular young performers by a mile. But with any touch of fame, it’s completely normal for questions to arise surrounding a particular performer — especially when it comes to a dazzling talent such as White. Of course, the fuel behind the fire of White’s skyrocketing popularity can be attested to his tremendous work on comedy-drama series The Bear — which released its second season on Hulu today.

As a result, fans of White are wondering who exactly White’s girlfriend is, and if he’s actually dating anyone at all. In the past, White has been linked to actress Emma Greenwell, who portrayed Mandy on Shameless. And while it’s hard to keep your personal life sacred as a celebrity, it’s even more difficult when you’re a buzzing star in the industry. 

Who is White’s girlfriend?

As it stands, White is legally married to actress Addison Timlin. The pair initially met back in 2008 while filming the movie Afterschool. The couple eventually formed a relationship years later, and ultimately got married in October 2019. A year prior, their first daughter was born, followed by the birth of their second daughter in December 2020. 

However, back in May, Timlin filed for divorce from White, indicating that the couple is struggling through some issues. Earlier in the year, White thanked Timlin during his acceptance speech at the Golden Globes. As of right now, the duo is separated, although neither party has publicly addressed the split.
